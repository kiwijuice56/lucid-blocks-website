frequently asked questions

  • what is this?

    lucid blocks is an entirely new way to experience your subconscious. it is open world, survival, and psychedelic. lucid blocks was made from scratch (in Godot); it isn't a mod of another game.

  • is it multiplayer?

    unfortunately, no. you must be entirely alone for the Qualia connection to function properly.
